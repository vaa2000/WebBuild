Human Team Foundation



About

This is an organisation which shares as much happiness as Possible to specially challenged children or the children who are struggling alone in this world!

Founded on 10 October 2015

Our Mission

We focus on making the maximum positive effort for our community. Our members and volunteers provide the momentum that helps us affect change.
" A world where every child counts; a world of children living in dignity."

Our History

Beginning from our engagement with a small group of college student helping one who is struggling alone in this world, our effort has always been to address deprivation and marginalization of communities and children. Over the years, health and child protection emerged as the major domains of our interventions, with a special focus on vulnerable children and their families.
